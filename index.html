Project D7012E

Ski-Resort Guide in HTML5

Our project in D7012E are to create a web page in HTML5 with some strict rules that have to be fulfilled to pass. We have choosed to create a site that are going to show Ski-resorts and give you the chance to see the differens between them so you find the the resort that are fitting just your needs, at the beginning we are going to start with one ski-resort that users are going to be able to uppload videos and pictures linked to the resorts slopes or just to the resort. Later on we are going to try to catch a feed from a resort to get the resorts upcoming events.
